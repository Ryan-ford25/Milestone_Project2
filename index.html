Rules

The rules for this game are simple.

  • The squares within the grid will light up in a random sequence.
  • Your task is to memorize this sequence and each turn relay it back by selecting the squares.
  • In this version you have three lives to start, if you fail to repeat the sequence correctly a life will be lost and the sequence will be replayed.
  • If your lives reach zero, you lose and will have to restart.
  • If you successfully relay the sequence then the game will progress to the next level.

Good Luck!
